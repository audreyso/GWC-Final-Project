Tests needed!!

Speaking Test:
  • Being able to speak to a United States Citizenship and Immigration Services Officer during your eligibility interview

    • Reading Test:
  • Being able to read a loud 3 sentences correctly in English

    • Writing Test:
  • Being able to write 1 out of 3 sentences correctly in English

    • Civics Test:
  • Being able to answer 6 out of 10 questions correctly regarding US History

    • Tips for taking your test!!!

  • Don't dress casual
  • Don't bring children
  • Review your application before the interview
  • Bring the correct documents:

    *Permanent Resident or Alien Registration card       *All valid and expired passports       *State Identification Card       *Any Re-entry Permits you have

  • Arrive an hour before your interview
  • Review flashcards the night before and day of
