Types of Tests

Speaking Test

-Being able to speak to a USCIS Officer during your eligibility interview

Reading Test

-Being able to read a loud 3 sentences correctly in English

Writing Test

-Being able to write 1 out of 3 sentences correctly in English

Civics Test

-Being able to anwser 6 out of 10 questions correctly regarding US History

Tips for taking your test!!!

-Don't dress casual

-Don't bring children

-Review your application before the interview

-Bring the correct documents

  • Permanent Resident or ALien Registration card
  • All valid and expired passports
  • State Identification Card
  • Any Re-entry Permits you have
